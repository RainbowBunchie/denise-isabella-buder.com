Turtlet

Turtlet was done by Alexander Tischhart, Sandra Zecha, Lukas Seiwald and I as graduation project for the master's degree.

Turtlet is a messaging app that counters the fast moving zeitgeist of our digital society by giving more emotional value and individualism to it's messages (so-called "Turtlets"). This is achieved by including the components time, place, personalization and uniqueness. Similar to a letter or postcard, Turtlets can only be sent to one person at a time and, depending on the distance between the conversation partners, take a certain amount of time to reach the recipient. The app is multimedia-based and also supports the integration of image content.

#ReactNative #JavaScript #Redux #Frontend #MobileApp

Soul Collectors

Soul Collectors was done by Alexander Tischhart, Lukas Seiwald and I as graduation project for the bachelor's degree.

Soul Collectors is a local multiplayer game that runs in the browser with the help of AirConsole and Phaser. The players have a shared screen in the browser window and use their smartphones as gamepads!

In Soul Collectors the players play against each other! When a player gets eliminated a soul appears over the dead body of the player. Now the other players' goal is to collect that soul and bring it back to their own base. But watch out, if the player gets eliminated while carrying a soul the soul disappears! If a player manages to collect a soul of every enemy player then he wins the round.
